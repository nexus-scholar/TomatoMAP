# Related Work Notes

## Tomato segmentation literature trend

Recent tomato segmentation studies continue to use models such as YOLOv8-seg and Mask R-CNN, often targeting ripeness recognition, mature fruit detection, robotic picking, or greenhouse operation. [web:32][web:33][web:35][web:53]

## Practical observation

This means that a paper framed only as "an improved segmentation architecture for tomato images" is harder to differentiate unless the gain is substantial and methodologically original. [web:32][web:33][web:35][web:53]

## Better positioning

A stronger paper angle is:
- label-efficient learning,
- semi-supervision,
- practical annotation scarcity,
- structured use of TomatoMAP.

## TomatoMAP relevance

TomatoMAP is presented as a multi-angle multi-pose tomato dataset with classification, detection, and segmentation components, which makes it suitable for future work beyond basic supervised segmentation. [web:39][web:40][web:43]

## Notes for future citation building

Potential citation groups:
1. TomatoMAP dataset descriptor and repository. [web:39][web:40][web:43]
2. Recent tomato segmentation papers using YOLO/Mask R-CNN. [web:32][web:33][web:35][web:53]
3. Broader review material on instance segmentation in agriculture or plant vision. [web:51]

## Narrative to maintain in writing

"Most prior work improves architectures under standard supervision; this work studies practical instance segmentation when tomato mask labels are limited."
