# Thesis Plan: 7 Months

## Overall thesis identity

The thesis should be framed as:

**Data-efficient tomato perception under annotation scarcity**

This is a stronger unifying story than several disconnected segmentation experiments.

## Proposed structure

### Chapter / Paper 1
Semi-supervised or label-efficient instance segmentation on TomatoMAP-Seg.

### Chapter / Paper 2
Cross-task transfer or weak supervision using TomatoMAP-Det and/or TomatoMAP-Cls if practical. TomatoMAP is described as a unified multi-task dataset, so this is a natural extension. [web:39][web:40][web:43]

### Chapter / Paper 3 or thesis chapter extension
One of:
- multi-view consistency,
- annotation-efficiency pipeline,
- active learning,
depending on what becomes realistic after Paper 1.
